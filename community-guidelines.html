Effective Date: July 23, 2026

1. Be Respectful

No harassment, threats, bullying, or targeted abuse.

2. Be Honest

No impersonation, fabricated credentials, or manipulation of reviews and ratings.

3. Review Fairly

  • Review the book, not the author's personal life.
  • No paid, exchanged, coerced, duplicated, or coordinated reviews.
  • Label spoilers clearly.

4. Respect Rights

Only upload content you own or are authorised to use. No piracy or stolen artwork.

5. No Harmful or Illegal Content

No illegal content, credible threats, doxxing, malware, scams, or sexual exploitation.

6. Promotion and Spam

Relevant promotion is allowed only where appropriate. Repetitive links and mass messaging are not.

7. Enforcement

Violations may lead to content removal, feature restrictions, suspension, or termination.

8. Reporting

Email indielibrarycustomerservice@gmail.com with the relevant link and explanation.
