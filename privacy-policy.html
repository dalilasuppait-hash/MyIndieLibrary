This Privacy Policy explains how Indie Library ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Indie Library website, mobile application, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, username, and profile details.
  • Author and book information, including book covers, descriptions, links, genres, themes, tropes, and other submitted content.
  • Reviews, ratings, comments, reports, and other user-generated content.
  • Transaction and subscription information if paid features are introduced.
  • Technical information such as IP address, device type, browser, operating system, app version, log data, and usage activity.
  • Communications sent to customer support.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create, secure, and manage accounts.
  • Display approved books, author profiles, and public contributions.
  • Provide search, discovery, review, and premium features.
  • Moderate content and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse.
  • Process payments and subscriptions where applicable.
  • Improve performance, usability, and reliability.
  • Respond to requests and legal obligations.

Book listings, author profiles, reviews, ratings, and other content submitted for publication may be visible publicly. Do not submit personal information you do not want to make public.

7. Cookies

Our website may use necessary cookies and, where enabled, analytics, preference, or advertising cookies. See our Cookie Policy.

10. International Transfers
