This page explains how users can request deletion of their Indie Library account and associated personal data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to indielibrarycustomerservice@gmail.com using the subject line:

Account Deletion Request

Please include:

  • The email address linked to your Indie Library account.
  • Your username or profile name, if applicable.
  • A clear statement confirming that you want your account deleted.

What will be deleted

  • Your account profile and login information.
  • Your name, email address, and profile details.
  • Your saved preferences and account activity.
  • Your book submissions, where removal is requested and legally permitted.
  • Your reviews, ratings, and other user-generated content, unless anonymisation is more appropriate.

Data that may be retained

Limited information may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, accounting, or enforcement of our terms. It will be kept only as long as necessary.

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days. We may contact you if additional information is needed to verify ownership of the account.

Delete specific data only

You may also ask us to remove specific content, such as a book listing, review, or profile detail, without deleting your entire account.
